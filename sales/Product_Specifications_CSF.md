1\. Material Genesis & Molecular Integration

Every complex compound found within biological fluids is derived from primary botanical production. Because the law of **Conservation of Mass** dictates that atoms cannot be created or destroyed, every protein string, carbohydrate ring, and lipid chain circulating within an animal or human is a rearranged plant molecule. Animals act as physical conduits that ingest, break down, and reassemble the elemental blocks originally bound together by photosynthesis.

This fluid bypasses animal intermediary processing by utilizing raw, un-centrifuged blue corn extracts extended with distilled water. When applied to an external or structural testing interface, the system receives direct plant-derived molecules and starches. These organic structures remain physically present at the interface, serving as an active moisture-retaining film until they are mechanically washed away or turned over by the surface's natural structural regeneration.

3\. Conservation of Mass in Systemic Fluid Baselines

In fluid modeling, the total mass of the system is rigidly conserved. When formulating this simulated CSF, the input mass of the distilled water solvent must exactly balance the dissolved plant solids and added sodium chloride salts. This specific baseline of fresh plant extract and pure distilled water is engineered to keep a structural testing model or skin surface hydrated, relying on the physical presence of bound water molecules to maintain surface volume and prevent structural collapse.

Phase Stabilization and Safety Operations Notice

To ensure these educational and product documents comply with established biological guidelines, these formulas are designated strictly for **external material science, educational lectures, and topical applications**.

Because the human vascular system lacks digestive capabilities and cannot process raw fats, un-emulsified waxes, or active soap detergents, any internal introduction of these materials will trigger severe, fatal vascular blockages (embolisms) or acute systemic shock. Keeping these mixtures pure, organic, and confined to external testing ensures complete compliance with product safety standards.
